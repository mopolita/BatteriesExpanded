# Batteries and Materials Expanded
A mod for Rimworld that adds a total of 39 batteries:
 - 4 sizes:
   - Small
   - Regular
   - Big
   - Giant
 - 9 types of batteries:
    - Lead
    - Copper
    - Nickel
    - Plated (include waterproof and precious ones)
    - Nickel
    - Uranium
    - Lithium
    - Plasteel
    - Ancient
